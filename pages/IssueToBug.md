The following article describes the process to report suspected issues and management of bugs once the issue is confirmed.

This process does not apply to issues within the team's scope of work. For example, when the software team identifies a software issue, the software team creates the proper work item within the assigned team area and works to resolve the issue.




## Issue Work Item
- Title (required): a short summary of the issue
- Assigned To: leave unassigned (triage to assign)
- Area Path: TEAM_S (triage to assign to team)
- Iteration Path: Current PI (assigned team to allocated to correct PI/sprint)
- Description: 
Instrument name and associated configuration (SW, FW, hardware version)
Steps to reproduce, what is the expected behavior vs. actual
- Attachment: Screen captures, video & logs if available.
- Priority: 1 (highest) - 4 (lowest)
- Due date: leave blank 
- Discussion: to be populated with investigation notes & data

## Bug Work Item
- Title (required):  a short summary of the issue
- Assigned To: unassigned (to assign at bug scrub to the team's PO)
- Area Path: TEAM_S (to assign at bug scrub) 
- Iteration Path: current PI (PO to assign to the appropriate PI/sprint)
- Repro Step: Steps to reproduce, what is the expected behavior vs. actual
- System Info: Instrument name and associated configuration (SW, FW, hardware version)
- Priority: how soon to address the bug, 1 (highest) - 4 (lowest)
- Severity: how severe is the impact on the user, 1 (critical) - 4 (low)
- Discussion: to be populated with bug scrub notes and development work
